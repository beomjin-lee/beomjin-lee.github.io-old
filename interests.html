Interests

My interests span computer science,
to puzzle-solving to playing table tennis

I am a computer science enthusiast

A bulk of my free time goes to reading more about computer science and the most recent trends. Most recently, I have been reading up on reinforcement learning, inspired by DeepMind's paper on AlphaGo Zero. I also enjoy making websites (like this one!) and just picking up new skills on the go.

I am a puzzle enthusiast

From the classic 3 by 3 Rubik's cube, to many of its variants, puzzles were always a great fascination for me. Since I picked up computer science, I have become very involved in Project Euler problems, which have increased both my mathematical and analytical intuition. If there are any other interesting puzzles, please share them with me!

I am a table tennis enthusiast

I have never really been good at sports, believe me - and alas, same applies for table tennis. However, what differentiates table tennis from other sports is how I have to stay vigilant at all times, making sure my reflexes are on top of my game, all the while strategizing the next move to beat the opponent. Exhilirating, to say the least!
